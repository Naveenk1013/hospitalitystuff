Introduction to Food & Beverage Equipment & Services

Tools, tableware, furniture, and service styles that define efficient and elegant hospitality operations.

  • Syllabus
  • Home

1. Introduction

F&B equipment includes tools, machines, and utensils used in preparation, cooking, serving, and storage. Services refer to methods like table service, buffet, and self-service.

2. Selection of F&B Equipment

Factors Affecting Selection

  • Type of establishment & service
  • Menu & cuisine
  • Durability, quality, standardization
  • Storage space
  • Ease of cleaning & maintenance
  • Aesthetic appeal
  • Availability & supply

Importance

  • Enhances efficiency
  • Maintains hygiene
  • Reduces cost
  • Improves guest experience

3. Furniture in F&B Service

Importance

  • Enhances theme & ambience
  • Provides comfort
  • Efficient space use
  • Brand identity

Types

  • Dining Tables: Wood, glass, various shapes
  • Chairs: Banquet, armchairs, bar stools
  • Sideboards: For cutlery, napkins
  • Trolleys: Gueridon, dessert
  • Bar Counter & Stools
  • Outdoor Furniture: Weather-resistant

Selection Factors

  • Comfort, durability
  • Design, aesthetics
  • Ease of cleaning
  • Space, budget
  • Material safety

4. Linen in F&B Service

Importance

  • Enhances appearance
  • Protects tables
  • Ensures hygiene
  • Reduces noise
  • Reflects standard

Types

  • Table Cloth
  • Slip/Silence Cloth
  • Napkin
  • Buffet Cloth & Skirting
  • Tray Cloth
  • Waiters Cloth

Care & Maintenance

  • Wash with mild detergent
  • Separate colors
  • Starch & iron
  • Store in ventilated room
  • Control system for issue/return

8. Coffee Shop Service

Characteristics

  • Casual, quick service
  • Extended hours (24/7)
  • Varied menu
  • Affordable pricing
  • High turnover

Menu Items

  • Beverages: Tea, coffee, juices
  • Breakfast: Omelets, sandwiches
  • Snacks: Burgers, fries
  • Main: Pasta, curries
  • Desserts: Ice cream, cakes

9. Cafeteria Service

Characteristics

  • Self-service
  • Display counters
  • Quick, low labor
  • Standardized portions
  • Cash/token payment

Types

  • Straight Line: Single queue
  • Free Flow: Multi-counter
  • Assist Service: Partial help
